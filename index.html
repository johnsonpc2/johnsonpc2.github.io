About

Welcome! My name is Pierce and I'm a cognitive scientist and doctoral student at the University at Albany, SUNY. Prior to moving to Albany, I graduated with a degree in Psychology and Philosophy from Elon University and spent a year as a lab manager at CU, Denver. You can learn more about my research, courses I've taught, and personal projects on this page. Please feel free to reach out if anything you see interests you. I'm always open to chat!

Teaching

My teaching centers the recognition that learning is demanding work. As a cognitive psychologist, I understand that efficiently acquiring information, integrating it with prior knowledge, and applying it appropriately requires deliberate practice from students and thoughtful design from an instructor. I care deeply about fostering curiosity in my students while simultaneously preparing them for the practical challenges they will face in the future.
