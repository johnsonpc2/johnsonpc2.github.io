Teaching

My teaching centers the recognition that learning is demanding work. As a cognitive psychologist, I understand that efficiently acquiring information, integrating it with prior knowledge, and applying it appropriately requires deliberate practice from students and thoughtful design from an instructor. I care deeply about fostering curiosity in my students while simultaneously preparing them for the practical challenges they will face in the future. Below, you'll find a history of the courses I have taught or been a TA for, along with the syllabus for the course, when possible.
